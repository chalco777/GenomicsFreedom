About Us

Welcome to GenomicsFreedom, an innovative platform designed for teaching genomics and creative exploration of genetic sequences.

Our mission is to provide an environment where you can not only learn the fundamental principles of genomic analysis, but also play with parameters and discover new patterns that you might not identify by following strict and conventional rules.

Unlike traditional approaches that limit analysis to specific reads, at GenomicsFreedom we encourage experimentation. For example, here you can apply k-mer distributions not only to reads, but also to entire genes, enabling discoveries that conventional methods might overlook.

Our platform combines professional tools with an elegant and intuitive interface, designed for researchers, students, and genomics enthusiasts seeking a more flexible and creative analysis experience.
